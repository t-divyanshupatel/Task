## How Does Bloom Work?

With Bloom, you can start selling in a matter of hours without writing any code:

1. **Describe Your Store:** Tell Bloom what you want to sell, how your store should look, and any special features you need.
2. **Let Bloom Build It:** Bloom creates a complete ecommerce store built on [Medusa](https://medusajs.com). This includes the storefront, admin dashboard, backend, and integrations.
3. **Refine and Customize:** Keep chatting to make adjustments. Change designs, layouts, and features until your store matches your vision.
4. **Go Live:** When you're ready, deploy with one click. Bloom handles hosting, SSL, and everything else you need to start selling.
