This guide covers how to change your profile information and reset your password.

<Note title="Tip">

This guide only covers managing your profile details. To manage your organization details, see [Manage Your Organization](../organization-management/page.mdx).

</Note>

## Update Profile Details

You can update your first name and last name in your profile settings.

To update your profile:

1. Open the [Bloom homepage](https://bloom.chat/).
2. Click on your Bloom organization name at the top.
3. Select "Account Settings" from the menu.
4. On the Profile page, update any of these fields:
   - **First name**: Your given name
   - **Last name**: Your family name
   - **Email**: You can't update your email. If you need to change your email, [contact support](../help-and-feedback/page.mdx#contact-support).
5. Click "Save changes" to update your profile.

## Reset Password

If you created your account with an email, you can reset your password if you forgot it or want to change it.

To reset your password:

1. Go to the [Bloom login page](https://bloom.chat/login).
2. Click "Reset" next to "Forgot password?"
3. Enter your email address.
4. Click "Send Instructions."

{/* TODO: Add image: Reset password form with email input and Send Instructions button */}

Bloom will send a password reset email to your inbox. Open the email and click the link to create a new password.

Once you reset your password, use the new password to log in to Bloom.

### Troubleshooting

If you don't receive an email to reset your password:

- Check your spam or junk folder.
- Make sure you entered the correct email address.
- [Contact support](../help-and-feedback/page.mdx#contact-support) if you still can't reset your password.
